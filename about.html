📊 About SAIClaws CPA

Free CPA exam study guides and resources — built to help you pass.

Our Mission

SAIClaws CPA exists to make high-quality CPA exam preparation accessible to everyone. We believe that cost shouldn't be a barrier to earning the CPA designation — one of the most valuable professional credentials in the world.

While major review courses charge $1,500–$5,500, we provide free, comprehensive study guides, strategies, and resources to help you understand what the CPA exam demands and how to prepare effectively.

🎯 What We Offer

  • Complete exam section guides — Detailed breakdowns of FAR, AUD, REG, and the 3 Discipline sections
  • Study strategies & methodology — Proven approaches based on how top scorers actually study
  • Free resources — Formula sheets, mnemonics, study planner frameworks, and practice questions
  • Honest course comparisons — Objective reviews of major CPA review courses to help you choose
  • Regular blog updates — Exam changes, study tips, and career guidance

What We're Not

We are not a paid CPA review course. We don't sell video lectures, question banks, or simulation software. We provide the free knowledge layer — the "what to study, how to study, and why" — that complements whatever paid course (if any) you choose.

If you're looking for comprehensive test prep with thousands of multiple-choice questions, task-based simulations, and adaptive learning technology, we recommend exploring the major CPA review providers. Our Study Resources page includes an overview of the best courses on the market.
