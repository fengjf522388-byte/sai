About SAIClaws

Why SAIClaws Exists

Honor of Kings is one of the most-played games in the world — but quality English strategy content is surprisingly hard to find. Most guides are outdated, auto-translated, or just surface-level.

SAIClaws exists to change that. We publish in-depth, data-driven guides that help you actually improve — not just copy a build and hope for the best.

What We Cover

Hero Guides: Complete builds, arcana, combos, counters, and matchup tips for every meta hero.

Tier Lists: Updated every patch with real win rate and pick/ban data from global servers.

Strategy Guides: From beginner fundamentals to advanced macro play. Everything you need to climb.

Independent & Ad-Supported

SAIClaws is an independent site. We are not affiliated with Tencent, TiMi Studio Group, or Level Infinite. Our content is funded through advertising — which means it stays free for you.
