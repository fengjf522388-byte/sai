⚡ Skill Order & Combos

Max Order: S1 → S2 → Ult (when available)

  • S1+AA+AA+S2 Poke combo. S1 for range boost, two auto-attacks, S2 to disengage.
  • AA+Ult+AA+S1+S2 Teamfight combo. Auto-attack to stack, Ult for AoE, S1 to reposition, S2 to escape.
⚠️ Positioning is everything on Loong. Stay behind your frontline and auto-attack from max range. Never face-check bushes.
