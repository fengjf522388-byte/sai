💡 Dyadia's healing scales with AP but she needs survivability. Build hybrid: Holy Grail for heals, tank items to stay alive in fights.

⚡ Playstyle

  • Stick to your ADC like glue. Your heal + shield makes them unkillable
  • Use Ult to save teammates from burst — timing is everything
  • Synergizes best with: Loong, Hou Yi (hypercarry marksmen)
  • Countered by: Anti-heal items (Blight). Ban or avoid if enemy builds it early
