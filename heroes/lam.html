⚡ Skill Order & Combos

Max Order: S1 → S2 → S3 (Ult when available)

  • S1+AA+S2+AABasic trade combo. S1 to engage, auto-attack, S2 for burst, auto-attack to finish. Ideal for lane ganks.
  • S2+S1+Ult+AAFull kill combo. S2 to close gap, S1 for slow, Ult to execute, auto-attack to clean up. Guaranteed kill on squishies.
  • S1+Ult+FlashFlashy execute. S1 to mark, Ult + Flash to reposition the execute angle. Hard to master but devastating.

🛡️ Counters & Threats

Strong Against: Marksmen (Loong, Hou Yi), Mages (Daji, Yixing) — can one-shot them with full combo.

Watch Out For: Tanks with hard CC (Kaizer, Lian Po), burst mages who can kite (Angela).

⚠️ Ban Priority: Lam is the #1 ban in ranked. If you main him, always request first pick.

📋 Pro Tips

  • Start Red Buff for maximum early pressure
  • Gank at level 2 — Lam's S2+S1 combo is deadly even without Ult
  • Save Ult for execute, not engage. Target enemies below 40% HP
  • Use bushes aggressively. Lam's burst is most effective from fog
  • Invade enemy jungle after first successful gank. Lam snowballs hard
