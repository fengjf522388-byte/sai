Da Qiao is the weirdest support in the game

Da Qiao can teleport her entire team across the map. Think about that for a second. She drops a portal (S2), teammates step in, boom — they're at her Ult location. At high elo, a good Da Qiao makes your team feel like they're everywhere at once.

The problem? At low elo, your teammates don't know what the portal does. You drop it to save them, they walk around it and die. Da Qiao is an S-tier hero in coordinated play and a B-tier hero in solo queue. Pick accordingly.

💡 Portal tricks

  • Drop S2 portal behind teammates when they're low. Don't put it IN the fight — put it somewhere they can walk back to.
  • Ult to objectives. Enemy team doing Tower Lord? Ult your team there and steal it.
  • Da Qiao + any global Ult hero (Hou Yi, etc.) is terrifying. Your ADC can recall to heal and teleport back instantly.
  • Don't first-pick her in solo queue. You need to know your team has a brain before locking her in.
🗺️ Da Qiao is the highest skill-ceiling support. Her win rate looks mediocre because people pick her without understanding macro. If you learn her properly, she's one of the most impactful heroes in the game.
