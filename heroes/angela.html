Angela: the "I just want to delete people" mage

Angela is Daji's slightly less popular cousin. Same idea — stun someone, press all your buttons, watch them disappear. Her S2 is a fireball that stuns, then you drop S1 (the fire rain thing) and Ult (giant laser beam). If the enemy doesn't have magic resist, they evaporate.

She's not meta-defining like Yixing, but she's way easier to play. If you're new to mid lane and Daji is banned, Angela's your answer.

💡 Quick tips

  • Combo order: S2 (stun) → S1 (damage) → Ult (laser). If S2 misses, back off. Don't commit.
  • Hide in bushes. Seriously. Angela is 10x more effective when enemies don't see you coming.
  • Your Ult makes you stand still. Make sure the enemy can't jump on you before you channel it.
  • Flash + S2 is a sneaky engage tool. Hard to react to. Use it to catch the enemy ADC off guard.
🔮 Angela vs Daji: Angela has slightly more AoE damage, Daji has better single-target burst. If the enemy team groups up a lot, pick Angela. If they have a fed carry you need to delete, pick Daji.
