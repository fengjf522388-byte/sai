💡 Yixing's strength is zone control. Use S1+S2 to block enemy paths, Ult to win teamfights. Hard to master but #1 mage when played right.

⚡ Game-Changing Ultimate

Yixing's Ult creates a massive zone that traps enemies. Use it to: split enemy team in teamfights, zone off objectives (Towers, Tyrant), or trap fleeing enemies for clean kills.

The key: cast Ult between enemy frontline and backline. This forces them to fight separated.

🛡️ Counters

Strong vs: Immobile teams, melee-heavy comps, any hero without dash escape.

Weak vs: High-mobility assassins (Lam), heroes with untargetable frames.
