🏹 Farm Lane (ADC) Guide

Marksman is the highest-damage role in the late game — but also the easiest to kill. Your job is to farm safely, show up to fights with items, and position well enough to not die immediately. Here's how.

1. Your life > one minion wave

I see this constantly in low elo: ADC walks up to last-hit a cannon minion, gets engaged on, dies, loses three waves anyway. If the enemy support is posturing aggressively, just let the gold go. It's not worth dying for. Your items will come — but only if you're alive.

2. Stand behind your support. Always.

You are not the frontline. You are a glass cannon. In teamfights, your job is to auto-attack the closest target from max range. If the enemy assassin dives you, your support should peel. If your support isn't peeling, that sucks — but don't compensate by playing like a tank.

3. Which ADC should you play?

Loong (S-tier, 55.3% WR) — Best ADC in the game. Scales insanely hard. Loong guide →

Hou Yi (A-tier, 50.9% WR) — Safe, reliable, easy. Perfect if you're learning. Hou Yi guide →

Marco Polo (A-tier, 51.5% WR) — High skill cap, high reward. Don't pick him below Diamond. Marco guide →

🏹 The #1 ADC mistake: face-checking bushes. You have the lowest HP in the game. Let your support check bushes. If your support won't do it, use a skill to check from range. Never walk into fog as an ADC.
